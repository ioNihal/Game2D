About

Welcome to Stickman Fighter! This is a fast-paced, endless 1v1 replayable fight game. Each round pits you against an opponent in a one-on-one duel: there are no levels or stages just you vs. another combatant in an endless series of matches. Hone your timing, master your moves, and see how many victories you can string together before defeat. Every fight is a fresh challenge!

How to Play

Use arrow keys or A/D to move left and right.

Press W or Up arrow to jump.

Press J to perform an attack.

Press K to block incoming hits.

On mobile devices, use the on-screen buttons for movement, jump, attack, and block.

Defeat your opponent to win the round. After each round, the next opponent appears see how many wins you can achieve in a row!

Game Features

• Simple stickman art style with responsive controls.
• Endless replay: each victory immediately leads into the next match.
• AI opponent with increasing difficulty (adjustable in settings).
• Mobile-friendly on-screen controls and desktop keyboard support.
• Sound effects and background music enhance immersion.

About the Developer

Hi, I’m the solo developer behind Stickman Fighter. I built this as a learning project and passion piece to explore 2D canvas-based gameplay, responsive input handling, and simple AI. My goal was to create a fun, replayable one-on-one fighting experience you can pick up quickly and play anywhere on desktop or mobile.

I hope you enjoy the game! Feedback and suggestions are welcome feel free to reach out or check any project repository or contact info you maintain.
