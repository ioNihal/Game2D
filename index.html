About

Stickman Fighter is a fast-paced 1v1 brawler. Beat your opponent across multiple rounds, first to 2 round wins takes the match. The AI will adapt: it defends, punishes whiffed attacks, and combos you.

Controls

Action Key
Move Left ← / A
Move Right → / D
Jump ↑ / W
Light Punch J
Heavy Punch U
Sweep Kick I
Block K (hold)
Pause Esc

Tips

• Block reduces incoming damage by 85%.

• Heavy Punch deals the most damage, save it for when the enemy whiffs.

• Sweep Kick hits low, good for mix-ups.

• The AI will punish your recovery frames on hard difficulty.
